Sorceror Class

You have an intuitive connection with magic. hit die: d6 proficiencies weapons: daggers, darts, slings, quarterstaffs, light crossbows saving throws: con, cha skills: choose 2 skills from arcana deception insight intimidation persuasion religion equipment a light crossbow or a simple weapon a component pouch or an arcane focus a dungeoneer's pack or an explorer's pack two daggers 4 sorcerer cantrips of your choice 2 level 1 sorceror spells of your choice 2 level 1 spell slots Your spellcasting ability is cha.

Sorcerous Origin

Choose a sorcerous origin.

Draconic Bloodline

You know the draconic language. If you are proficient in some cha skill and use it while interacting with dragons, you are considered expert. You have draconic resilience: your max hp increases by 1, and it increases by 1 every time you gain a level. As well, if you are not wearing armor, your AC is 13 + dex modifier.

Wild Magic

You are prone to wild magic surges: from time to time the DM will inform you of random magical effects you accidentally loose. Tides of Chaos: You can manipulate the forces of chance and chaos to gain advantage on one attack roll, ability check, or saving throw. Once you do so, you must finish a long rest before you can use this feature again.
