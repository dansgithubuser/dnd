Dwarf Race

Beers, beards, battleaxes, and stalagmites. You're a dwarf. Most dwarves believe in order and fair play. +2 con age: 50 to 350 height: 4 to 5 weight: about 150 speed: 25 languages: common, dwarvish You are uninhibited by heavy armor whose strength requirement exceeds your own. You have darkvision. You have dwarven resilience: advantage on saving throws against poison and resistance against poison damage. You have proficiency with battleaxes, handaxes, light hammers, and warhammers. You have proficiency with one of smith's, brewer's, or mason's tools. You are stonecunning: you are considered an expert when making a history check related to stonework.

Subraces

Dwarves are further split into subraces; pick one.

Hill Dwarf

+1 wis You have dwarven toughness: your max hp increases by 1, and it increases by 1 every time you gain a level.
