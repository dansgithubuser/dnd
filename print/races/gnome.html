Gnome Race

Gnomes tend to be playful inventors, explorers, and investigators. Some gnomes call their home (typically a burrow) their garden. +2 int age: 40 to 350 height: 3 to 4 (unlike the other commonly playable races, you are considered small) weight: about 40 speed: 25 languages: common, gnomish You have darkvision. You have gnome cunning: you have advantage on mental saving throws against magic.

Subraces

Gnomes are further split into subraces; pick one.

Forest Gnome

+1 dex You know the minor illusion cantrip. Intelligence is your spellcasting ability for it. You can speak with small beasts.

Rock Gnome

+1 con If you are proficient in history, when you make a history check related to magic items, alchemical objects, or technological devices, you are considered an expert. You have proficiency with tinker's tools.
