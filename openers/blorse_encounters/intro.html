Blorse Encounters of the 4th Kind

You are an adventurer who lives on Fenristan. Fenristan is a human colony built mostly in harmony with wolfkind natives. The wolfkind tend to the northern forests, and the Fenristan people live in the southern plains.

The Jormungangr are a society of seafaring orcs who inhabit the local seas. They are antagonistic to everyone. Land-based nations in the area have less trade and war than you'd otherwise expect.

You have never known it to snow in Fenristan, and yet you wake to a fresh layer of snow. The local wizard claims it is a sign and summons adventurers to sail southeast and "fix the tree". He offers 2000 gold up front and promises powerful magical items to be present at the destination. As well, he gives you a scroll of greater restoration to use on the tree, and a scroll of revivify, just in case.

Rockholm is a dwarven city famous for its hammersmiths. You can get any hammer you need: artisinal, magical, war hammers, and stone splitters. For the right price, you could find a hammer for any and every occasion.

New Hel is a small human establishment known for its academy specializing in Necromancy. There is also a library with a map collection.

Florfinthel is a small elven establishment. The trees that grow here are well-suited to making magical bows and arrows. The elves tend to barter for things with stories, or sometimes just a story.

The black dragon Linx ranges the area. He sleeps for years at a time and usually isn't a problem. When he has been a problem, he tends to eat wolfkind and have treasure stolen from the dwarves.
