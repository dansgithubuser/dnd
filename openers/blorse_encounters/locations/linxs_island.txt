The adult brass dragon Linx slumbers in the northeast island, in ruins made by humans long ago.

While Linx sleeps, she has a passive perception of 18.
If Linx wakes, she will attack the party until they surrender.
Linx will forgive the trespass in exchange for treasure or promise of treasure (worth 1000 gold equivalent or more).

Linx treasure contains:
- 15000 gp
- 1400 pp
- 5 works of arg worth 250 gp each
- vorpal longsword
