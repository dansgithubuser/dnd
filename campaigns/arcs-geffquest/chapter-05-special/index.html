notes characters

Flashback

  • Marcus Ancestor is with his family, expecting his brother. Let player introduce the scene.
  • Brother arrives. Let player describe.
  • Their callstones ring. Something is wrong. They have to report to central.
  • Through the gate, to the link between planes, to central where they are debriefed.
  • Something-like-a-dragon has arrived and started wreaking havoc. The wizards have been deployed.
  • A town suffers collateral damage and they are dispatched to rescue survivors.
  • Town is mostly demolished, survivors need to be dug out of the rubble, healed.
  • Too much time passes without a note from central. Let the players describe an act of kindness.
  • Eventually, the story gets to them: the link was sacrificed to kill the dragon.
  • 14 years go by. Let the players figure out how they met each other.

Fallen Prophet

  • The Prophet, Ex, dies and self-resurrects. Regardless, the next prophet, Tensa, is activated. The self-resurrection is seen as blasphemous.
  • Ex claims he had a vision while dead, and that everyone was doomed. He starts a conquest, claiming to offer compassionate death.
