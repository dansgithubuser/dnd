===== Summary =====
While still in Sigirroeth, Geff gets a letter from his uncle to come help at his earliest leisure. His uncle is the leader of a land not so far from Gethbridge. Geff seeks favor with various politicians. Effy does not like Geff's uncle.

Geff's uncle wants to manipulate the party into dismantling neighbor nation's military, so that he can claim the whole landmass. He will install Geff's sister as a minister to stoke Geff's jealousy. He will stage an assassination of the sister if necessary.

Spies of opposing nation are present in Geff's uncle's nation.

Geff's dad is the black sheep / runt of the litter. The rest court power.

===== Hook =====
Geff gets a letter from his uncle Althion, the king of Akola, to come help at his earliest leisure.

===== Development =====
In Akola, Althion explains that their northern neighbor Doshku is ruled by a power-hungry government that cares nothing for its people. The military has tightened their border patrol and disallow key trading keeping both nations people healthy. Altion asks Geff and his party to cut through part of the border and allow trade to continue.

As well, Hilari has been employed as minister of culture. Her stated goal is to develop the culture of Akola. Altion's has other ideas of her use. She keeps people entertained and uninterested in the conflict with Doshku. She is a vehicle of jealousy that will encourage Geff to dismantle the Doshku military and government. Althion will offer higher political position depending on how much Geff accomplishes.

Hilari's final purpose is to be kidnapped by a Freefolk mercenary group, hired by Althion, and taken to Doshku, enraging Geff.

===== Conflict in Akola =====
Akola's northern neighbor, Doshku, is governed by an isolationist group, the Sons of Dosh. They came to power a decade ago. Their military patrols the border and prevents trading between the people of Akola and Doshku. The Sons of Dosh want to conquer Akola. Minorities of people agree or oppose the Sons of Dosh. Most don't really care, though they know their government is primarily focused on maintaining and growing power.

Akola controls tropical forests and exports spices, fruits, furniture, clothing, and medicines.

Doshku controls mountains and exports minerals, tools, and jewelry.

Both are mostly-human settlements. They are the only two countries on the landmass and both claim the whole landmass is also called by their country name.

===== Secrets =====
Akola's sewers are home to a displaced group of people, the Freefolk. They spy on Akolans and trade information to the Sons of Dosh. The Freefolk also inhabit caves in Doshku.

There is a mage tower just north of the border. Ostensibly Doshkan mages spy on both countries. They are interested in a form of lichdom that leaves the soul intact, and require children to continue their experimentation. They kidnap orphaned children who no one is keeping track of.

Althion is not interested in peace. He also wants to conquer Doshku, for notoriety's sake.

==== Altion's relatives =====
Altion's wife Ava was Akola blood royalty, but she passed away, and Althion became acting king. They had three children.

Prince Althoro, the eldest, is meant to inherit kingdom. He has been of age for a couple years, but Althion has convinced him not to accept the title yet, because times are difficult.

Prince Chod is a kindly young man that has no interest in politics. He is a keen cook.

Princess Larriana, the youngest, plays the part of princess and is adored by the people. She spins Althion's actions in a good light, but has the same ambitions as her father.

Althion is eldest of four siblings. Geff's father is the youngest. The middle two are Agnes von Juckel and Andra Glass, both married to power and queens of their respective lands.
