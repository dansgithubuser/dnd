While still in Sigirroeth, Geff gets a letter from his uncle to come help at his earliest leisure. His uncle is the leader of a land not so far from Gethbridge. Geff seeks favor with various politicians. Effy does not like Geff's uncle.

Geff's uncle wants to manipulate the party into dismantling neighbor nation's military, so that he can claim the whole landmass. He will install Geff's sister as a minister to stoke Geff's jealousy. He will stage an assassination of the sister if necessary.

Spies of opposing nation are present in Geff's uncle's nation.

Geff's dad is the black sheep / runt of the litter. The rest court power.
