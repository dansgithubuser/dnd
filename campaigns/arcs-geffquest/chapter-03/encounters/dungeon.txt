===== mother grick =====
Armor Class 15 (natural armor)
Hit Points 84
Speed 30 ft.

STR 16 (+3) DEX 14 (+2) CON 12 (+1) INT 5 (-1) WIS 14 (+2) CHA 5 (-3)

Damage Resistances Bludgeoning, Piercing, and Slashing from Nonmagical Attacks
Senses Darkvision 60 ft., Passive Perception 12
Languages --
Proficiency Bonus +3

Regeneration. regains 5 hit points at the start of its turn. If the grick takes acid or fire damage, this trait doesn't function at the start of the grick's next turn.
Stone Camouflage. The grick has advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks made to hide in rocky terrain.

Multiattack. The grick makes one attack with its tentacles. If that attack hits, the grick can make two beak attack against the same target.
Tentacles. Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 9 (2d6 + 3) slashing damage.
Beak. Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 10 (2d6 + 3) piercing damage.

===== grick kid =====
This wormlike monstrosity blends in with the rock of the caverns it haunts. When prey comes near, its barbed tentacles unfurl to reveal its hungry, snapping beak.

Armor Class 14 (natural armor)
Hit Points 27 (6d8)
Speed 30 ft., climb 30 ft.

STR 14 (+2) DEX 14 (+2) CON 11 (+0) INT 3 (-4) WIS 14 (+2) CHA 5 (-3)

Senses Darkvision 60 ft., Passive Perception 12
Languages --
Challenge 2 (450 XP)
Proficiency Bonus +2

Stone Camouflage. The grick has advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks made to hide in rocky terrain.

Multiattack. The grick makes one attack with its tentacles. If that attack hits, the grick can make one beak attack against the same target.
Tentacles. Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 9 (2d6 + 2) slashing damage.
Beak. Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 5 (1d6 + 2) piercing damage.
