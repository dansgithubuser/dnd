=====Half-Orc Race=====
You probably don't want to talk about how your parents came to conceive you. Your orc parent likely either left you before or shortly after you were born; your human parents can't deal with your aggressive tendencies. You're also probably infertile.

+2 str
+1 con
age: 14 to 75
height: 6 to 7
weight: 180 to 250
speed: 30
languages: common, orc

You have darkvision.
You have proficiency at intimidation.
You have relentless endurance: when you drop to 0 hp and are not killed outright, you can drop to 1 hp instead. You can't use this feature again until you finish a long rest.
Your attacks are savage: when you score a critical hit with a melee weapon, you can roll one of the weapon's damage dice again and add the result to the damage total.
