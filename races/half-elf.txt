=====Half-Elf Race=====
Despite your parents promise to be unlike every other human-elf parents they've heard of, your elf parent thinks you're a boor, and you bully your human parent. You're also probably infertile.

+2 cha
+1 to two other ability scores
age: 20 to 180
height: 5 to 6
weight: 100 to 160
speed: 30
languages: common, elven, one of your choice

You have darkvision.
You have fey ancestry: you have advantage on saving throws against being charmed, and magic can't put you to sleep.
You are proficient in two skills of your choice.
