=====Tiefling Race=====
You're a demon-looking humanoid. Generations ago, your human ancestors made a deal with a devil.

+2 cha
+1 int
age: 18 to 85
height: 5 to 6
weight: 100 to 180
speed: 30
languages: common, infernal

You have darkvision.
You have resistance to fire damage.
You know the thaumaturgy cantrip.
When you reach 3rd level, you can cast hellish rebuke as a 2nd-level spell. You cannot do this again until you finish a long rest.
When you reach 5th level, you can cast darkness. You cannot do this again until you finish a long rest.
Charisma is your spellcasting ability for the aforementioned spells.
